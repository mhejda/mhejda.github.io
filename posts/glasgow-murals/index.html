The Murals of Glasgow (ultimate list)

February 29, 2020 804 words 4 min

Street art and street murals are becoming a staple feature of many different cities around the globe - including Glasgow, the largest Scottish city. Colourful artistic paintings of various sizes can be found all around the city, and there’s even an official mural trail to help you discover them. There’s just one small issue with the officia trail: there’s many more really nice murals than what is included in the trail (and new murals keep popping up). After randomly discovering couple of nice murals in unexpected areas of the city, I came up with the idea to create my own “ultimate” Glasgow mural list, to be updated everytime I find a new one. :)

Note: The numbering of those is arbitrary, and doesn’t at the moment constitute a good order of visiting the murals.
