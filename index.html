I’m Matěj Hejda.

⠀

⚫ I am a member of research staff in the Large-scale Integrated Photonics (LSIP) group at Hewlett Packard Labs, where I work as an Architecture & System Design Research Scientist. My research focus is on optical computing architectures, particularly for AI acceleration and neuromorphic computing. Nowadays, I am based in Brussels, Belgium 📍. 󠁧󠁢󠁳󠁣󠁴󠁿

⚪ I have completed my PhD in Dr Antonio Hurtado’s group at Institute of Photonics, University of Strathclyde in Glasgow, UK, where I investigated neuron-like dynamics in lasers and optoelectronic devices for neuromorphic information processing. Previously, I have completed my Master’s project in the Optoelectronic group lead by Prof Valerio Pruneri at ICFO, Barcelona.

⚫ Outside of research, I deeply enjoy music (particularly electronic), and produce my own electronic songs. I also enjoy travelling, learning new things as well as tinkering with Python and personal knowledge management tools. Read more…
